What we found digging into the data

Three things the team doesn't know yet

These jumped out immediately from the Shopify admin. Each is a quick fix with outsized impact.

01 Critical

~22% of sessions are bot traffic

Data-center IPs from Ashburn (629 sessions), Council Bluffs (584), and unattributed China (696) together = ~1,900 sessions. Your real CR on human traffic is already 1.14%, not 0.89%. This also corrupts every metric and A/B test result.
Fix (15 min)Filter Ashburn + Council Bluffs in GA4. Add Cloudflare bot protection.
02 Critical

Stale 2025-calendars page is #2 landing page

/collections/2025-calendars pulled 462 sessions in Q1 2026. Visitors arrive, see last year's sold-out stock, and leave. Same for /collections/diwali-collection (109 sessions in April).
Fix (30 min)301-redirect to evergreen collections. Google re-crawls in 2–4 weeks.
03 High

All 5 hero calendars are 100% sold out

Typewriter, Toaster, Sewing, Postcard, and Wall calendars all at full sell-through. The theme still features calendars prominently, but April buyers can't convert on them. This is an inventory + merchandising problem, not a design one.
Fix (2 hrs)Foreground in-stock gifting + home décor until August. Start 2027 calendar waitlist.

The plan, on a timeline

The 90-day roadmap

Four phases. Each has measurable targets. Designed around actual traffic (no A/B testing nonsense at 9K sessions).

Week 1–2

Diagnose

  • Install Microsoft Clarity + GA4 ecommerce tracking
  • Watch 30 session recordings (PDP + checkout)
  • Read 100 support tickets, tag by theme
  • Launch post-purchase survey ("What almost stopped you?")
  • Build weekly KPI dashboard (this one!)
  • Filter bot traffic (Ashburn + Council Bluffs) from GA4
Week-2 checkpointTop 10 friction moments documented · Clean analytics baseline
Week 3–4

Quick wins

  • 301-redirect /2025-calendars · hide Diwali collection
  • Add pincode + COD checker on every PDP
  • Enable Shop Pay + UPI intent at checkout
  • Confirm guest checkout is ON
  • Free-shipping progress bar in cart drawer
  • Trust row + social proof on every PDP
  • Sticky mobile ATC · Compress hero images to WebP
  • Install post-purchase upsell (ReConvert)
Day 30 checkpointCR ≥ 1.2% · ATC ≥ 4.5% · +20–30% revenue
Week 5–8

Content + Structural

  • "Shop by Occasion" megamenu (Birthdays, Housewarming, Weddings, Kids)
  • Rewrite top 10 PDP copy · benefit-first + objection handling
  • 3 new gift-guide blogs (expand the miniature-books hit)
  • "Why Sky Goodies" page · founder story + FSC + reviews
  • WhatsApp abandoned cart + order updates (Interakt/Wati)
  • Launch 3 new bundles (Home Décor Starter, Kids' Birthday, Housewarming Trio)
Day 60 checkpointCR ≥ 1.5% · AOV ≥ ₹1,250 · Returning ≥ 40%
Week 9–12

Retention + Paid

  • Klaviyo flows: Welcome, Browse abandon, Cart, Post-purchase, Win-back
  • SMS flow for COD confirmation (reduces RTO 20–40%)
  • Small Meta/Google retargeting test (₹500/day · 30 days)
  • Launch referral program ("Give ₹100, get ₹100")
  • 2027 calendar waitlist (open by August)
Day 90 checkpointCR ≥ 1.8% · +₹85K incremental revenue · Sustainable CRO rhythm
