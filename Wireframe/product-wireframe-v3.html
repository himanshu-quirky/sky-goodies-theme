Description

These Paper Mini Lanterns are a hands-on, screen-free activity that brings colour and warmth to any space. Each lantern is designed as a geometric shape that lets light filter through in a soft, diffused glow. String all 10 lanterns on the included LED fairy lights and hang them across your bookshelf, balcony, or living room for an instant mood lift.

This is not just a decor product — it is an experience. The process of folding and assembling each lantern is calming, creative, and deeply satisfying. Once done, you have something truly handmade to call your own.

What's Included
  • 10 pre-cut lantern sheets (premium 300 GSM art paper)
  • 1 LED fairy light string (10 warm white LEDs, battery-operated)
  • Illustrated instruction booklet with QR code for video tutorial
  • Assembly clips to secure each lantern
How to Make
  1. Fold along the pre-scored lines — Each lantern sheet comes with score lines already pressed in. Just fold along them to form the lantern shape. Takes about 2 minutes per lantern.
  2. Clip the edges together — Use the included assembly clips to hold each lantern in its 3D shape. No glue needed, no mess.
  3. Thread the LED string through — Slip one LED bulb into each lantern, string them all up, and switch on. You are done.
