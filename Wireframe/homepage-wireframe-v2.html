Our story

Paper can spark something wonderful.

In 2013, Amit and Misha left their corporate careers with a simple idea: that paper could do more than sit in a printer. What started as a small experiment in their Mumbai apartment is now a studio that has put 50,000+ handcrafted kits into homes across India.

Every kit is designed at NID, printed on premium recycled paper, and made to bring people together -- one fold at a time.
