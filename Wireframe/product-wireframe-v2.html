Description

Transform any space with these charming miniature paper lanterns. Each kit comes with 10 pre-cut lantern sheets in vibrant colours, a warm white LED fairy light string, and a step-by-step guide. No scissors, no glue, no craft degree required.

The lanterns are made from premium 300 GSM art card that holds its shape beautifully. Once assembled, they create a warm, ambient glow that works equally well in a child's bedroom, on a balcony, or as festive decoration.

Designed at our Mumbai studio. Made in India. 100% recyclable.

What's in the Box
  • 10 pre-cut paper lantern sheets (5 colours x 2 each)
  • 1 warm white LED fairy light string (3m, battery operated)
  • 2 AAA batteries
  • Illustrated instruction card with QR code for video tutorial
How to Make

Scan the QR code on the instruction card or visit our YouTube channel. Each lantern takes about 3 minutes to fold. The entire set takes 30-45 minutes to assemble. Pro tip: put on your favourite podcast.
