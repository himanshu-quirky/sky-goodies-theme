The Sky Goodies story

Paper can spark something wonderful.

In 2013, Amit and Misha Gudibanda — both NID-trained designers, life partners, and co-dreamers — left successful corporate careers to chase a simple idea: that the joy of making something with bare hands never gets old.

Every kit is designed in Mumbai using eco-friendly paper, clear instructions, and colourful illustrations inspired by Indian street art and truck art. From a five-year-old folding a first paper animal to a grown-up assembling a gramophone for the bookshelf — that "I made this!" feeling is what Sky Goodies is all about.
