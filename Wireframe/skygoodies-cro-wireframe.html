Our Story

Born in a small Mumbai studio, in 2013.

Amit and Misha — both NID-trained designers — started Sky Goodies with a simple belief: paper has the power to pause a hurried day. Every kit is illustrated, die-cut, and quality-checked under one roof.

A decade on, our paper lamps light homes in Tokyo, our calendars hang in Brooklyn apartments, and our Ganesha idols grace pandals in Pune. But the joy is the same as on day one.
